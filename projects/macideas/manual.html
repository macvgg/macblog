How do I save my tasks?

Don't worry about saving your tasks on MacIdeas, because we've got you covered! Everytime you create a new task or edit one, we'll automatically save everything, so nothing could possibly get lost!

How do I save my notes?

MacIdeas autosaves your notes every 10 seconds, ensuring your progress is saved every once in a while when jotting down an idea or information! This prevents you from losing too much progress if you accidentally close out the tab. Other than autosave, you can also use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + S to save your progress manually, which prevents you from losing any progress at all! A small notification pops out from the bottom right when the note saves both manualy or automatically.
